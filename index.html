October 03-06, 2019 Belgrade, Serbia.

PyCon Balkan is an annual conference, organized by Python Belgrade Organization, that takes place in the Balkan region each year since 2018.

The key aim of PyCon Balkan is to connect the region and provide a real-time meeting opportunity for developers and companies from the region and internationally. We thrive on helping the growth of the diverse Python community in the Balkans, promoting Python programming language, and becoming a place where you can learn new things and improve yourself professionally.
