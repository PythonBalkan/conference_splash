Welcome to PyCon Balkan!

PyCon Balkan is an annual event that is being held in various countries in the Balkan region since 2018.

Key aim of PyCon Balkan is to connect the region and provide a real-time meeting opportunity for developers and companies from this region and internationally.

We are currently working on our official website, so this is just a temporary webpage. If anyone is interested to help us with the website, please apply to volunteer. We would appreciate your help. :)
